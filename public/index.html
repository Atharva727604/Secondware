Warranty & Service Policy

🛡️ Warranty Coverage

Because these are warehouse-surplus items, the original manufacturer warranty is void. However, SecondWare stands behind every product we sell with our own localized protection:

  • 1-Year Comprehensive Warranty: Provided on all standard electrical components and products.
  • 3-Year Compressor Warranty: Applicable to all refrigeration units and air conditioners.

🔧 Exclusive Nagpur Delivery & Service

To ensure the highest quality of care and reliable support, we operate exclusively within the Nagpur region. Our delivery and on-site service are focused on providing the best experience for local residents.

🔄 Return & Delivery Policy

We strive for transparency. Since our products are sold at significantly discounted rates due to their "warehouse-surplus" status:

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are calculated based on your shipping area and product category (bulky items like fridges have higher rates). These are shown during checkout.

Inspection at Delivery

We encourage all customers to thoroughly inspect the appliance upon delivery before accepting it.

Functional Guarantee

If the product has a functional defect upon installation, we offer an immediate replacement or repair.

Final Sale

Due to the deep discounts offered, we generally do not accept returns for "change of mind" or minor cosmetic marks that were disclosed at the time of purchase.

⏱️ Order Cancellation

Orders can only be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order. After this window, cancellations will not be accepted.
